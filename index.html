FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trustly really completely free?

Yes! Trustly is 100% free forever with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or feature limitations. We believe every Discord community deserves access to professional-grade security tools regardless of size or budget.

What permissions does the bot require?

Trustly requires standard moderation permissions including Manage Messages, Ban Members, Kick Members, and View Channels. We only request permissions that are essential for protection and security features.

How does the global ban database work?

When you ban a user for serious violations, their information is anonymously added to our encrypted shared database. Other servers using Trustly receive real-time alerts when flagged users attempt to join, creating a network effect that protects everyone.

Will this impact server performance?

No. Trustly is engineered for maximum performance with sub-second response times and minimal resource usage. Our distributed infrastructure ensures your server runs smoothly with zero latency impact.

Can I customize the bot's behavior?

Absolutely! Trustly is highly customizable with flexible rule engines, custom filters, configurable automated actions, adjustable sensitivity levels, and personalized notification preferences to match your community's needs.

How is my server data handled?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols. We never sell or share your data with third parties. You can request complete data exports or deletion at any time through our privacy portal.
