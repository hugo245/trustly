Introduction

Our Commitment to Privacy

Trustly respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

We collect information such as server IDs, user activity logs (anonymized), and threat reports to provide security services. Data is used solely for threat detection and improvement of our database.

  • Data Sharing: We do not sell your data. It may be shared with law enforcement if required.
  • Security Measures: All data is encrypted using AES-256 standards.
  • Your Rights: You can request data deletion or access via support@trustly.example.

For questions, contact our Data Protection Officer.
